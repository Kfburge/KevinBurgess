Regional Honors Conference 2017 Speaker
 In 2017, I had the honor of being a speaker of the 45th South Regional Honors Conference(SRHC) in Asheville 2017. The SRHC is a conference held every year that focuses on highlighting the academic work of college honor's students in the Southeast US. My talk was on Japanese Isolationism from 1600 to 1853 A.D.
