Square Enix Case Study - Japanese Business

A case study project for JAPN 4990 Japanese Culture. This study seeks to assess the current strengths and weaknesses of the Square Enix corporation. This study pulls from various sources of data including Square Enix's sales, production costs, shareholder reports and consumer reception to paint a clearer picture of the comapanies current state of affairs.



Vagabond Report - Japanese Literature

A research project on the Japanese historical fiction Vagabond written by Inoue Takehiko. This project analyzes the plot and characters as well as the cultural context of Japan's Edo period.



International Industry Awareness Brochure - International Marketing


This project is a brochure I designed on recent developments in the international market for MKT 4270 International Marketing. Focus is put on emerging technologies such as blockchain and 3D printing and their potential effects on international trade. Some attention is also given to the international trade job market and potential prerequisities one needs to work in the international trade field.



US Soda Consumption Case Study - Managerial Economics


Here is a short case study on soft drink consumption demand across the continential US I wrote for ECON 3060 Managerial Economics. Using Excel I ran a regression and calculated the demand function for soft drink consumption and quanitified the individual variables and their statistical significance to soft drink consumption. My results and interpretations on the data are included.



Understanding the Nature of Japanese Loanwords - Japanese Linguistics

This is my final research paper for JAPN 3980 Language in Japanese Society. In this research paper I take a look into how Japanese loanwords have evolved and developed into their modern day usage. I also take a look at how the loanwords are used and view by Japanese people today and future change that may occur as a result of those viewpoints.
